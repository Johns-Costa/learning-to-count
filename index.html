Game Title: Learn to Count

-------

Objective: The objective of the game is to arrange a set of randomly displayed numbers in the correct numerical order.

-------

Rules:

  1. The game begins with the player or team selecting Level 1.
  2. The numbers 1 to 10 (or 20 depending on the level selected) will appear on the screen in a random order.
  3. The player or team must arrange these numbers in ascending order, as quickly as possible.
  4. Simply tap or click on it to select a number.
  5. Continue arranging the numbers until they are all in the correct order.
  6. If playing with a timer, the player or team can try to complete the task within a certain time limit for an added challenge.
  7. Once all numbers are selected, a player should press check to see if the game is won!
